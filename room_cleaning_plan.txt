Project: Room Cleaning Plan
Difficulty: Medium

Steps:
- Declutter and remove all items that don't belong or are trash (start with surfaces, then floor).
- Strip bed linens and put them in the laundry.
- Dust all surfaces, starting from top (ceiling fan, light fixtures) and working your way down (shelves, dressers, nightstands).
- Clean mirrors and windows with glass cleaner.
- Wipe down all hard surfaces (desks, tables, shelves) with an appropriate cleaner.
- Make the bed with fresh linens.
- Vacuum or sweep and mop the floor.
- Empty and clean the trash can.
