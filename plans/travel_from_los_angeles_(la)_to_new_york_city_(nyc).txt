Project: Travel from Los Angeles (LA) to New York City (NYC)
Difficulty: Medium
Created at: 2024-07-29T12:00:00Z

Steps:
- Define travel dates and budget.
- Research transportation options (flights, train, bus, car) and compare prices/travel times.
- Book preferred transportation (flights are generally fastest and most common).
- Book accommodation in NYC (hotel, Airbnb, hostel, or stay with friends/family).
- Create a packing list based on NYC weather and planned activities.
- Pack luggage, ensuring all essential documents (ID, tickets) are easily accessible.
- Arrange transportation to the LA departure point (e.g., LAX airport).
- Travel to NYC.
- Arrange transportation from NYC arrival point (e.g., JFK, LGA, EWR airport) to accommodation.
- Check into accommodation.

Estimated hours: 24
