Project: Bedroom Refresh
Difficulty: Medium

Steps:
- Gather cleaning supplies (trash bags, dust cloth, all-purpose cleaner, vacuum/mop, laundry basket).
- Open windows for ventilation.
- Remove all trash and place into a trash bag.
- Pick up all items from the floor and surfaces. Sort into piles: 'keep', 'put away', 'donate', 'laundry', 'trash'.
- Put away 'put away' items in their designated spots.
- Place 'laundry' items into the laundry basket.
- Empty the 'donate' bag/pile into a designated donation box.
- Dust all surfaces from top to bottom (ceiling fan, light fixtures, shelves, window sills, furniture).
- Wipe down all hard surfaces (tables, dressers, nightstands, desk) with an all-purpose cleaner.
- Clean mirrors and windows with glass cleaner.
- Vacuum or mop the floor, moving furniture where possible to clean underneath.
- Make the bed (change linens if necessary).
- Empty and clean the waste bin.
- Rearrange any furniture or decor as desired.
- Freshen the air with a room spray or diffuser.
